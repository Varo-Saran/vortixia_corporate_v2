# Vortixia Asset Generation Blueprint

The following prompts are specifically tuned for Midjourney v6 to generate hyper-realistic, high-end corporate and technological assets that perfectly align with Vortixia's neo-brutalist and brutal-glass aesthetics.

## Prompt 1: Hero Section Abstract Architecture
**/imagine prompt:** `Cinematic hyper-realistic architectural photography of a brutalist tech headquarters interior. Floor-to-ceiling frosted glass panes intersecting with raw concrete pillars. A single sliver of neon volt-yellow light (#CCFF00) reflecting off a polished obsidian floor. Minimalist, sharp lines, zero curvature. High contrast, low-key lighting, Sony A7R IV, 35mm lens, 8k resolution, architectural digest style --ar 16:9 --style raw --v 6.0`
